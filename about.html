Our Journey

Precision Engineering is Our Core

FILTEC Polyplast has stood as a reliable name in high-performance polymer extrusion. From our state-of-the-art facility located in Kendrapara, Odisha, we cater to municipal contractors, large agricultural concerns, and global plumbing networks.

We believe that B2B projects require more than just pipes—they require strict compliance with dimensional guidelines, prompt supply lines, and components that will last for generations.

By utilizing the latest German extrusion heads and rigorous raw material selection, we ensure our pipelines outperform generic alternatives in tensile strength, pressure thresholds, and chemical resistances.

Testing Frameworks

Our in-house quality lab is built around testing equipment that measures multiple properties of the pipe, including:

  • check_circle
    Hydrostatic Pressure Resistance:

    Pipes are subjected to high internal water pressures at elevated temperatures for sustained periods to check long-term burst strength.

  • check_circle
    Carbon Black Content & Dispersion:

    Ensuring proper UV protection for outdoor agricultural pipeline layouts through pigment homogeneity analysis.

  • check_circle
    Reversion & Longitudinal Shrinkage:

    Ensures that thermal expansion doesn't compromise joint integrity under varying weather conditions.

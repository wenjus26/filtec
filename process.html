The Manufacturing Lifecycle

6 Steps of Precision Sizing

1
Step 01

Material Selection

We source high-grade polymer resin and utilize advanced additive blending to ensure thermal stability and chemical resistance.

2
Step 02

Precision Extrusion

Advanced German-tech extrusion lines melt and shape the polymer, maintaining uniform wall thickness across every millimeter.

Zero Seepage Commitment

Quality Testing Lab Procedures

Every batch of piping conforms to the NABL certification framework. We verify durability using destructive and non-destructive measures.

verified_user

Tested and stamped under ISO 9001 and Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) parameters.

1. Hydrostatic Burst Test

Subjecting randomly selected pipes to pressures up to 3 times their rated limit at 80°C, verifying material molecular bonds stay unbroken.

2. Online Thickness Inspection

High-speed ultrasonic scanner logs diameter coordinates in real-time, preventing weak spots or wall thinnings across the extrusion sequence.

3. Impact & Heat Reversion Testing

Dropping weighted hammers at 0°C to check impact resistance, and baking samples to ensure low longitudinal shrink thresholds.

About the Company

Who We Are

FILTEC Polyplast Pvt Ltd is a fast-growing manufacturer of high-quality PVC, uPVC, and CPVC pipes & fittings designed for modern plumbing, water supply, irrigation, drainage, and industrial applications.

Established with a vision to deliver durable, reliable, and cost-effective piping solutions, FILTEC combines advanced manufacturing technology with strict quality standards to serve residential, commercial, agricultural, and infrastructure sectors across India.

Our manufacturing facility at Bhubaneswar, Odisha is strategically located to ensure efficient logistics, easy availability of raw materials, and seamless supply chain management.

Testing Frameworks

  • check_circle
    Hydrostatic Pressure Resistance:

    Pipes are subjected to high internal water pressures at elevated temperatures for sustained periods to check long-term burst strength.

  • check_circle
    Carbon Black Content & Dispersion:

    Ensuring proper UV protection for outdoor agricultural pipeline layouts through pigment homogeneity analysis.

  • check_circle
    Reversion & Longitudinal Shrinkage:

    Ensures that thermal expansion doesn't compromise joint integrity under varying weather conditions.
